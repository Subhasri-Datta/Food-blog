My Favorite Cuisine and Signature Dish: Creamy Garlic Alfredo

This recipe is perfect for beginners and experienced cooks alike. It’s rich, creamy, and bursting with flavor.

Ingredients (Serves 2-3)

  • 250g of fettuccine pasta (or any pasta of your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Tips for Perfect Pasta Every Time

  1. Salt the Water: Salting the pasta water is key to flavorful pasta. It should taste like the sea!
  2. Don’t Overcook: Keep the pasta al dente, as it will continue to cook slightly when mixed with the sauce.
  3. Save Pasta Water: The starchy water helps thicken and bind the sauce to the pasta.
  4. Use Fresh Ingredients: Fresh garlic, Parmesan, and parsley make a huge difference in flavor.
